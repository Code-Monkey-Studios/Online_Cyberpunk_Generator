Cyberpunk™

Online Character Generator

Welcome to my Cyberpunk 2020™ 2nd ed 0nline Character Generator. This is written entirely in Javascript & jQuery. On the next page you will find a slider of books you may have or have access to, just scroll through and enable the books you'll be using and the generator will include them. the next page after that is the generator itself in a tabbed panel just work your way through the tabbed sections and at the end press the Generate Printable button if at anytime you are unhappy with your character just press the Reset button which will reset the generator and return you to this page. If you get lost there are plenty of tool tips to help you along the way. Scrolling to the next page will show you a printable version of your character if you are happy with your character you can, at this point, print it by pressing Generate Printable from the Equipment tab then selecting print from the file menu of your browser.

Neo Tribes

In 2020s America, the urban sprawls that seem to stretch on forever are dwarfed by vast expanses of open country, abandoned after the collapse. Areas once choked with cities and humanity are now home only to lonely ribbons of asphalt, coyotes, jackrabbits... And the Neotribes.
